Get your kettles, pumps, and temperature controls integrated seamlessly by professional welding and electrical technicians.

Standardized Sanitary Installations

Any minor scratch or microscopic leak in a weld can collect bacteria, leading to ruined batches and sour beers. That is why our installation service uses premium TIG welding techniques under argon gas shields, ensuring perfectly smooth inner walls in every pipe connection.

What the Installation Includes:

  • Manifold Mountings: Perfect routing of mashing, filtration, boiling, and whirlpool fluid paths.
  • Glycol Chiller Integration: Seamless distribution to all jacketed fermenters with manual and solenoid control overrides.
  • Hydrostatic Pressure Testing: Every line is pressure checked for 24 hours to guarantee zero liquid leakage under operation.
  • Control Panel Calibration: Tuning PID algorithms to prevent temperature overshoot during wort heating and boiling.
