Our Legacy & Passion

Designing precision craft tools since 2012 for those who view beer not just as a beverage, but as an art form.

From Garage to Commercial Brew House

Our journey began in a small garage in Oregon with three homebrewers, two kettles, and one simple mission: build equipment that offers homebrewers the exact sanitary standards and temperature accuracy of major commercial breweries.

Today, BrewCraft supplies professional craft breweries and hobbyists across fifteen countries with tri-clamp stainless steel connections, glycol chillers, and certified fresh hops and malts.

2012

BrewCraft founded with our first patented copper counter-flow wort chiller.

2017

Launched the Pro-Series Conical Fermenters with double-jacketed glycol lines.

2023

Established the BrewCraft organic hop farm and recipe consultation services.

Our Core Values

Every kettle weld and grain batch is shaped by three guiding principles.

Absolute Sanitary Design

Using only 304 food-grade polished stainless steel to prevent bacterial nesting and off-flavors.

Thermodynamic Precision

Designing custom coil distributions that guarantee uniform temperatures within 0.5 degrees.

Shared Open Science

Publishing all our base recipes, grain proportions, and calculation sheets open-source for the brewing community.
