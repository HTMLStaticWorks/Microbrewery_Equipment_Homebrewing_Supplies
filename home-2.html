Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the correct fermenter size?

Select a vessel that leaves at least 20% headspace above your standard batch size. A 7-gallon conical fermenter is ideal for active 5-gallon wort fermentations to avoid krausen blowoff.

What is the advantage of glycol jacketing?

Glycol jackets circulate cold coolant through double-wall stainless steel layers. This allows you to regulate fermentation temperatures to within 0.5 degrees, eliminating off-flavors.

Can I automate my step-mashing schedules?

Yes, our Pro-Series Digital Brew Controllers automate heating elements to execute steps (acid rests, protein rests, saccharification) according to user-programmed timings.
